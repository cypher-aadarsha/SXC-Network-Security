Who We Are

The SXC Network Security Community is a student-led initiative at St. Xavier's College, dedicated to fostering a culture of cybersecurity awareness and technical excellence. Our community is built on the principles of collaboration, continuous learning, and ethical security practices.

We provide a platform for students to explore the vast and dynamic field of network security, from ethical hacking and penetration testing to cryptography and digital forensics.

  • ✓Hands-on workshops and training sessions.
  • ✓Engaging Capture The Flag (CTF) competitions.
  • ✓Talks by industry experts and professionals.
  • ✓Collaborative projects and research opportunities.
